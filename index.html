Lavatory Logger

Problem

The problem we have chosen to tackle with this project is the ongoing issue of vandalism in our school bathrooms which have lead to the closure of many of the schoolâ€™s bathrooms, leading students to walk across campus to the nearest bathroom that is open. Cleaning the bathrooms takes too much time for the janitors and it takes too long for students to use the bathrooms during class.

Solution

Our solution is to implement an app for a tablet or a small scanner for students. The students will scan their ID cards when they enter and exit the room to go to the bathroom. We plan to make a prototype for a small scanner using a Raspberry Pi. The time the student leaves and returns to the classroom will be recorded and saved to a database. School administrators will have access to a website where they can look up who was in the bathroom at a certain time or when a certain person went to the bathroom. Doing this will help catch the person who is vandalizing by finding a correlation between occurrences of vandalism and who was in the bathroom at the time.
